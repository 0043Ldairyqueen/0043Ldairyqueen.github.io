CONTACT US

DQ restaurants are independently owned and operated franchised locations that have served customers for over 70 years. The independent franchise operators of DQ® restaurants and their employees, together with International Dairy Queen Corporation (IDQ), strive to provide customers with the best products and service possible. Your feedback is important because it enables Do® franchise owners and IDQ to ensure that they maintain high standards of products and service.

If you have comments regarding a particular DQ® restaurant, please contact the independent franchise operator of that location directly so that they may personally and immediately assist you. Use the store locator to obtain the contact information for that restaurant.

You may also use the link below to provide feedback to IDQ regarding one of its franchised restaurants, and IDQ will forward your comments directly to the independent franchise operator of that location for you.
